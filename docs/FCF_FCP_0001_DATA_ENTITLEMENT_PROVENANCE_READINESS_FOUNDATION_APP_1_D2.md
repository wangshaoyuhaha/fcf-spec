# FCF FCP 0001 Data Entitlement Provenance Readiness Foundation App 1 D2

Status: COMPLETE

## Objective

Register immutable source entitlement records and calculate deterministic,
fail-closed coverage findings for exact fields, intended use, market scope,
rights evidence, retention, lineage, and registered evidence.

## Delivered

- source-keyed immutable entitlement registry
- duplicate source and record identity rejection
- deterministic canonical registry SHA-256
- explicit missing-record and not-researched handling
- exact required-field coverage comparison
- intended-use permission verification
- market scope, rights, retention, lineage, and evidence coverage findings
- stable finding order independent of input order

## Non-Authorization

D2 reports evidence readiness only. A complete coverage result is not a license
approval, source selection, phase authorization, or gap closure. FCF-FCP-0001
remains NEEDS_RESEARCH and Operator review remains mandatory.
