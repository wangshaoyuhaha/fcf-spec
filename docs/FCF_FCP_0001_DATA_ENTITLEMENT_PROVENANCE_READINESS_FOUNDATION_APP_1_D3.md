# FCF FCP 0001 Data Entitlement Provenance Readiness Foundation App 1 D3

Status: COMPLETE

## Objective

Calculate deterministic operational readiness findings for freshness, latency,
cost, expiry, revocation, and their registered evidence references.

## Delivered

- dimension-specific retention, service-level, cost, expiry, and revocation
  evidence references
- explicit missing freshness and latency objective findings
- explicit missing cost and cost-evidence findings
- explicit missing retention and service-level evidence findings
- expired, approaching-expiry, perpetual, and not-researched expiry handling
- active, revoked, and not-researched revocation handling
- stable operational finding order

## Non-Authorization

D3 evaluates registered metadata without contacting a source or vendor. It
does not approve cost, rights, a license, a data feed, a product phase, or a
future-readiness gap. FCF-FCP-0001 remains NEEDS_RESEARCH and Operator review
remains mandatory.
