# V2-R35 D2 Integrity Contracts

Registered evidence records carry canonical fields, a recomputed SHA-256 digest,
point-in-time timestamps, explicit OBSERVED or INFERRED origin, revision number,
and predecessor lineage.

Inferred records require a registered derivation identifier and source record
hashes. Observed records cannot carry inference lineage.

P1-P47 frozen. No P48. Operator review is mandatory.
