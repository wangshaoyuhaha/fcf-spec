# PAPER-VALIDATION-RUNTIME-APP-1 D4

## Scope

D4 adds deterministic validation result and Operator review packets.

## Capabilities

- preserve comparison blocking reasons
- preserve review reasons
- preserve explicit risk flags
- preserve contradiction records
- propagate run_id and correlation_id
- force blocking status for blocking risk evidence
- force blocking status for unresolved high or critical contradictions
- expose only paper-review Operator actions

## Authority

- deterministic comparison remains calculation authority
- registered artifacts remain evidence authority
- Operator review remains mandatory
- AI remains advisory only

## Prohibited

- no automatic approval
- no automatic promotion
- no automatic baseline replacement
- no automatic learning activation
- no automatic archive
- no real execution
- no broker or exchange connection
- no credential, account, balance, position, or wallet access
