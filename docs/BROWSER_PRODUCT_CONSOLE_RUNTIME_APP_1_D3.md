# BROWSER-PRODUCT-CONSOLE-RUNTIME-APP-1 D3

## Status

IMPLEMENTED

## Scope

D3 implements the browser product shell, navigation, and read-only stock
workspaces.

Implemented routes:

- Overview
- Stock Candidates
- Evidence and Risk
- Paper and Shadow Validation
- Operator Review
- Reports and Archive
- Health

The stock workspace displays ranked candidates, score breakdowns, reason codes,
risk flags, data quality, and confidence. Write methods are rejected. No buy,
sell, order, broker, exchange, account, position, wallet, promotion, or
automatic approval control is exposed.
