# V2-R1 Factor Contract Foundation App 1 D1

Status: IMPLEMENTED

D1 establishes the durable future-capability intake and the closed V2-R1
authority boundary. New proposals retain permanent identifiers and review
history but cannot authorize a phase, data source, factor, model, or route.

The boundary is paper-only, local-only, loopback-only, sidecar-only,
registered-artifact-only, read-only, and Operator-reviewed. Deterministic
Engine and Registered Evidence remain authoritative. AI remains advisory.

P1-P47 frozen; no P48. The production factor runtime remains not implemented.
No broker, exchange, credential, account, balance, position, wallet, order,
execution, tag, release, or deployment path is added.
